FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about OACEC, our services, and how we can help you with your offshore safety reporting and compliance needs.

Who is the app designed for?

The platform is designed for offshore oil and gas operators, HSE departments, and contractors who need an easy way to capture and manage safety observations.

Can this be used outside oil and gas?

Yes. While built for offshore, the system can be adapted for construction, mining, or any sector with high safety reporting needs.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Crews can submit cards without a connection. Once connectivity is restored, the data automatically syncs.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted, stored securely, and access is role-based. For sensitive environments, we also offer private cloud or on-premise deployments.

How quickly can we get started?

Most clients are up and running within a few days. We provide onboarding and training to make the transition smooth.

Do you customise forms for different companies?

Yes, we can customise forms to meet the specific needs of different companies. Our platform is designed to be flexible and adaptable.

How many users can the system handle?

The platform scales from a single rig to corporate-wide deployment with thousands of users.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. We provide a 3-month pilot programme for one rig or site. The first month is free, with no obligation to continue.

How is pricing structured?

Pricing is flexible — per rig, per user, or enterprise-wide. We’ll tailor it to your needs.

Are there hidden fees?

No. All costs are transparent. Optional add-ons like integrations or white-labelling are clearly priced.

More Questions? Contact Us

OACEC is a provider of digital solutions for the offshore industry, specialising in safety reporting and compliance tools. Our mission is to enhance safety and efficiency through innovative technology that empowers teams to act quickly and effectively.
