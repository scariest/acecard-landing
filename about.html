Who is OACEC?

We are a team of engineers and safety professionals who believe technology should make offshore operations not only more efficient, but safer for everyone. That’s why we built the Observation Awareness Communication Elimination Card Reporting Platform. A simple, mobile-first solution that makes it easy for crews to log safety observations and for managers to act on them immediately.
